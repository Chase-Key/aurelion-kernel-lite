## 🏛️ Foundation-Focused Name Options

### ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Option 1: ATLAS

**What it is:**
- Greek Titan who holds up the celestial spheres
- Architecture: Male figure supporting a structure
- Metaphor: Foundation that supports everything above

**Meaning for AURELION:**
- "The foundation that holds up your professional world"
- Architectural term (fits with 5-floor library metaphor)
- Suggests comprehensive mapping (atlas = collection of maps)

**Pros:**
- ✅ Strong foundational metaphor
- ✅ Architectural/technical term (not just planning)
- ✅ Memorable and distinctive
- ✅ Works with tagline: "Your Professional Atlas"
- ✅ Better trademark position than generic terms
- ✅ Suggests both structure (Titan) and navigation (maps)

**Cons:**
- ⚠️ Some tech usage (MongoDB Atlas, Atlassian Atlas)
- ⚠️ But always combined with brand (AURELION ATLAS is unique)

**FTO Risk:** ⚠️ MEDIUM (common in tech, but "AURELION ATLAS" is clear)

**Trademark Search Notes:**
- "Atlas" widely used but always prefixed
- "AURELION ATLAS" would be distinctive
- Class 9 (software) likely available with proper branding

**Repository Names:**
```
aurelion-atlas-personal
aurelion-atlas-premium
aurelion-atlas-business
```

**Usage Examples:**
- "AURELION ATLAS is your strategic planning foundation"
- "Using the ATLAS module to map my career progression"
- "The ATLAS 5-floor architecture organizes everything"

   - Works with 5-floor library metaphor

3. **Distinctive:**
   - More memorable than "Foundation" or "Framework"
   - Better trademark position

4. **Marketing Taglines:**
   - "Your Professional Atlas"

5. **FTO Safety:**
   - Medium risk alone, but "AURELION ATLAS" is distinctive
