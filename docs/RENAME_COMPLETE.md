## Naming Rationale

### Why ADVISOR?

**User's requirement:** "CK is just trying to advance his career with tier 1 open-source rollouts"

**Analysis:**
- ❌ PLANNER: Too plain, sounds like calendar app
- ❌ STRATEGY: Too vague, unclear purpose
- ⭐ **ADVISOR**: Perfect balance of sophistication + clarity

**ADVISOR wins because:**
- ✅ Implies intelligence, expertise, guidance
- ✅ Clear purpose (strategic advice for career/projects)
- ✅ Sophisticated enough for career showcase
- ✅ Not vague or generic
- ✅ Scales across tiers: Personal (templates), Premium (AI-enhanced), Business (team advisory)

### Why AAI (from AAAI)?

**FTO Issue:** "AAAI" is trademarked by Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence

**Solution:**
- AAAI → AAI
- Stands for: Autonomous Agentic AI
- Still pronounceable, distinctive, clear
